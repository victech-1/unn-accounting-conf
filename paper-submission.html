Paper Submission

You can publish the manuscript and deliver paper presentation on ACCIC-UNN , orally or by poster presentation. You must submit Full-length Manuscript for review before the online submission deadline.

An accepted proposal for a single paper presentation (prepared by one or more authors) will be assigned to one of the following formats by the Program Committee:

  • Physical Presentation
  • Visual Presentation

    • You can participate and attend all the presentation sessions only, neither making presentation nor having paper published.
