UNEC

Address: University of Nigeria, Enugu Campus

UNEC is situated in the heart of downtown Enugu with unparalleled access to many of the attractions in the vibrant Enugu. UNEC convenient location facilitates exploration and discovery.

Driving Directions

Take cab. Turn right and drive along straight towards ‘centrum’, all lights straight until the sign for the. That will lead you to Okpara Square (circular way towards the bridge). Take inner lane and follow signs to UNEC into the street.
