Registration Instruction

Step 1: Login to the website "www.acctyiconference-unn.org" then click on create account bottom(as a first timer)

Step 2: fill in your details and other information as required and click "Create Account"

Step 3: login with the email address and password as provided in step 2.

Step 4: go to registration bottom click either "listener's registration" or "Author's registration"

N.B; Listener's registration is for someone that is not presenting any paper, while Author's registration are for lead author's and co-author's that are presenting paper.

Step 5: fill in the details as provided after clicking any of the registration type in step 4

Step 6: click submit after filling in the details in step 5.

Step 7: after successfully submitting your abstract in step 6, it will show pending until is accepted after peered review. N.B; after accepting your abstract, a mail would be sent to you directing you on how to make the payment with the payment details.

Step 8: after making the payment , go back to the website and upload the payment evidence under registration, you would see "upload receipt"

Step 9: the full paper should be uploaded under "paper submission" after payment. N.B under paper submission, you would see "upload full paper"
